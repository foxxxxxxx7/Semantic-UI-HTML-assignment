December 2019 Update

by Robert Fox(20088034)

Our friendly and professional Leisure Centre staff will be delighted to assist you both in the gym and the pool. Our 20-metre deck pool has dedicated swim only lanes for those keen to practice their lengths or to improve their general fitness. We have a separate toddler pool for the kids and we also offer a number of classes such as aqua aerobics, parent toddler swimming classes as well as adult and children's swimming lessons.
Our gym is equipped with technogym cardiovascular and resistance equipment and our staff are more than happy to answer any questions you may have or indeed to create a special fitness programme to suit you and your needs.
